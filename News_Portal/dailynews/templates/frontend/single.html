Several devotees have reportedly died in the stampede that broke out at the Sangam area of the Maha Kumbh on Wednesday. Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has said that some devotees sustained serious injuries. Crores of pilgrims jostled for space to take a holy dip on the occasion of Mauni Amavasya. The incident took place between 1 am and 2 am when some devotees climbed over the barricades at Akhara marg, he said.

The tragic incident occurred when a barrier collapsed, triggering chaos and panic among the crowd, according to Akanksha Rana, the special executive officer for the festival. The stampede occurred as people rushed to participate in the sacred bathing ritual, a central event in the Maha Kumbh Mela.This year, a rare celestial alignment called 'Triveni Yog' is occurring after 144 years, amplifying the spiritual significance of the day. The 'Amrit Snan' on 'Mauni Amavasya' is expected to draw around 10 crore pilgrims.
